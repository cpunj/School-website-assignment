Unit details

Unit Unit Description Campus Semester Unit co-ordinator
MEC2034 Mechanical Science This unit introduces the fundamental concepts of mechanical engineering science. Students develop knowledge and understanding of static and dynamic equilibrum of mechanical engineering systems. It also provides an introduction to core areas of mechanical engineering, including thermodynamics and aerodynamics. Harrison Wells
CHEM231 Chemical engineering Chemical science is the study of matter. In this collection, you will find educational resources covering subjects such as pure mixtures and substances, states of matter and properties of materials. Teaching resources available include worksheets, posters, teaching presentations, classroom decorations and more. Caitlin Snow
AI235 Artificial Intelligence The unit provides an introduction to many AI sub-fields, including: expert systems, machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, intelligent agents. Students will be exposed to state-of-the-art examples as well as emerging technologies and get practical experience of solving interesting problems in each of these sub-fields. The unit covers the definition of Artificial Intelligence and its subfields; introduces foundational logic and knowledge representation; and considers social, ethical, and philosophical consequences of the theory and practice of AI. Students will learn different perspectives on the approach to creating AI, its purpose, and its validity. Cisco Ramone
